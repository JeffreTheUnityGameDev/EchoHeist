◈ HOW TO PLAY
THE ECHO HEIST — QUICK REFERENCE
① THE LOOP
🔄
You have a 10-second loop. Every move you make is recorded.
When the loop ends, you restart — but a ghost Echo of your last run replays beside you.
Stack Echoes to hold multiple switches at once.
② OBJECTIVE
🏁
Get ALL pressure plates held down at the same time.
The locked door turns green and opens.
Walk through to reach the EXIT ◈ and win.
⚠ SENTRY TURRET
Tracks & fires at the nearest entity — player or Echo.
Bullets bounce once off walls.
Hitting you = instant death and full restart.
Echoes absorb hits without dying.
☠ VOID TILES
Purple-hatched tiles are instant death for everyone — player and Echoes alike.
There is no recovery. Avoid them.
❄ ICE FLOOR
Blue crystalline tiles are slippery — you build momentum and can't stop instantly.
Great for crossing gaps quickly, dangerous near walls or voids. Your Echo is unaffected.
◎ STICKY FLOOR
Amber goo tiles slow you to a crawl — every step is a struggle.
Use a Launch Pad to escape, or just survive long enough to walk out. Your Echo is unaffected.
③ ITEMS — drag from your hand onto the grid during setup
SHIELD
Blocks bullets. Place it between you and a turret to walk past safely.
LAUNCH PAD
Launches you over walls in a parabolic arc. Scroll wheel on it to rotate direction.
MIRROR
Deflects bullets 90°. Scroll on placed mirror to flip its angle (\ or /).
PRISM WALL
Splits 1 bullet into 3 spread shots. Great for hitting multiple targets at once.
TIME SLOW
Creates an area-of-effect zone. Bullets passing through it slow to half speed.
TELEPORTER
One card = two warp points A + B. Place A, then B auto-appears to place. Step on A → warp to B.
DECOY
Turrets always target decoys first — ignore everything else. Place it to move freely while it draws all the fire.
